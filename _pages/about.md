Before coming to Columbia, I was at MIT. I received a BS in Electrical Engineering and Music. For a MS, I was at MIT Media Lab's Opera of the Future group where I experimented with interactive sonic experiences and helped produce acoustic+digital hybrid musical performances. My master's thesis was an interactive AR experience titled <em>Freedom Simulator</em>.

At lost hours, you will find me, with high probability, playing piano or reading. Some of my favorite musical eras are minimalism (Cage, Glass, Ziporyn, Eastman, Satie), impressionism (Ravel), and spectralism (Stockhausen, Crumb).
